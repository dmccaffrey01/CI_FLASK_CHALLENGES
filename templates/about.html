The Peaky Blinders is a renowned British crime drama television series that first premiered in 2013. Set in post-World War I Birmingham, England, the show revolves around the Shelby family, led by the charismatic and cunning Thomas Shelby, played by Cillian Murphy. The Shelby family, known as the Peaky Blinders, are notorious for their illegal activities, including bookmaking, smuggling, and street fighting. With their signature flat caps and razor blades sewn into their peaked caps, the Peaky Blinders have become synonymous with their daring and dangerous reputation. The show is known for its gripping plotlines, complex characters, and dark and gritty atmosphere, as well as its stylish period setting and iconic fashion. As the Shelby family navigates the treacherous criminal underworld, they face rival gangs, political intrigue, and personal challenges, making for a compelling and addictive viewing experience that has captivated audiences worldwide.
